Good typography enhances readability, establishes hierarchy, and contributes to the overall mood of a design. Key principles include contrast, repetition, alignment, and proximity.

Successful font pairings often create contrast while maintaining harmony. A common approach is pairing a serif with a sans serif, using one for headings and another for body text.

Web typography requires consideration of readability across devices, loading performance, and accessibility. Variable fonts and responsive typography are modern approaches to these challenges.
